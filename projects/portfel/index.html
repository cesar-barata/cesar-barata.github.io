Portfel

26 November 2019

Portfel is a wallet management service.

Concept

The concept is very simple: a user creates an account and then they can create as many wallets as they want which are of course associated with their account.

Initially, the wallets will provide very elementary functionality: they will be simply collections of deposits and withdrawals.

Purpose

The main purpose of the project is research and experiment with software architecture, engineering techniques, programming paradigms, software development and testing practices and so on.

Development

This section will be written and improved as the project is developed.

More information

I intend to keep a kind of journal of this project's development here on the blog. If you're interested look for blog posts with the portfel tag.

The whole idea was conceived by me and my friend Kleber Stender, so you can go to his site and check his take on this project.
